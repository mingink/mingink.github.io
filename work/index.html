Work

BrightWise is a young but potential software develop company which founded by me and my partner Hani. We provide high quality custom software development services to our corporate clients in mainland China and Macau. Recently, we have also tried to develop financial consulting business.

We customized and developed Macau’s largest private clinic appointment management system CareU for Macau customers.We have also served large state-owned enterprises, startups, and well-known Internet companies.

Team : rich industrial experiences

We are from top universities in China, like Fudan University, U of Macau, Beijing Forestry University and so on. Most of members in our team have more than 5 years industrial experiences. So we can provide our customers with professional and high-quality solutions.

Service : focus on software development and startup financial consulting

We are good at the development of mobile Internet software, including H5, applet, native APP, ERP system. We can provide a full set of development services for the entire process, or only part of the process, such as UI design, front-end develop, back-end develop, technical consulting, etc.

We also provide financial consulting for startups, and we are especially good at tax consulting. Our consultant is Certified Tax Agents (CTA) in Mainland China.

Advantage - cost-effective, flexible, honestly

Based on the characteristics of software development, we use remote office, so there is no need to bear unnecessary costs such as house rent. At the same time, most of the technicians are located in cities in mainland China such as Wuhan, and the labor cost is relatively low. Therefore, we can provide cost-effective but very reliable service.

More importantly, we are a flexible team, willing to take into account the various special circumstances of our customers and adjust our solutions accordingly.

Being honest with customers has always been an important principle for our team to do business. No matter when and under any circumstances, we will abide by this principle.
